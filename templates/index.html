About Oral Cancer

Know the facts, detect early, save lives

Common Causes

Tobacco use, excessive alcohol consumption, HPV infection, and prolonged sun exposure to the lips are the leading causes of oral cancer.

Symptoms to Watch

Persistent sores, lumps or growths in the mouth, difficulty swallowing, changes in voice, and unexplained weight loss.

Prevention

Regular dental check-ups and self-examinations of the mouth can help identify any unusual changes or symptoms at an early stage.
