Welcome to Oral Cancer Detector

Oral cancer, also known as mouth cancer, is a type of cancer that can develop in the oral cavity, including the lips, tongue, gums, cheeks, and throat. It's a type of head and neck cancer.

It is important to note that early detection and treatment are crucial for improving the prognosis of oral cancer.

Regular dental check-ups and self-examinations of the mouth can help identify any unusual changes or symptoms.

Common causes: tobacco use, excessive alcohol, HPV infection, and sun exposure to lips.
